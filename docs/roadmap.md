# Roadmap & Vision — MUN MIND

> Guides exploratory work and keeps development aligned with long-term goals.

## Vision

**One-liner:** _[To be defined]_

**North star:** An anonymous, low-friction mental health check-in tool that MUN students actually use — and that the university can maintain long-term.

## Guiding Principles
- Anonymity and trust above all
- Simple enough for a university IT team to run
- Free/low-cost infrastructure only
- Accessible to all students
